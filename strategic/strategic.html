Strategic Games

Welcome to the strategic category, this category is for those looking to use more brain juice or who gets a kick by outplaying or outsmarting your opponents, the strategic games are certainly the one you should be looking for. There are many games ranging from 30 minutes to hours, and in this particular category given the depth and level of decision making required in these types of games, expect longer wait times between turns and in an orderly turn based fashioned. However the rigidness of the game allows for alot more variation and replayability as compared to the other 2 categories aimed at something light and easy to play but inevitably falls short to what tabletop games can truly offer, an experience that can arouse heated discussions and always plenty to talk about, expansions are generally not necessary as the game itself is already very rich.
