Role-Playing Games

Welcome to the Role-playing category, the main theme of these games is building up a character and combating other players and monsters, or even going on quests together. Others include mystery guessing games, with many roles each achieving a different purpose. For those who wish to be placed in a different time era and play a game similar to dungeons and dragons, this would be a good place to start looking, there plenty of games giving you hours of fun and with your friends and family.
