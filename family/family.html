Family Games

Welcome to the family category, here's what to expect, easy to learn rules that can be learnt in 5 minutes and after that its nights of endless fun and laughter. This category is designed to showcase games that are suitable for family or friends who just want something casual and light without being too competitive, a common theme of family games is the more open ended style of play that does not have a step by step outcome but rather uses player creativity and imagination with a charm of luck to win the game, ultimately the main goal of the game in this category is not to win but to have fun.
